Cookie Policy

Last Updated: March 15, 2024

1. Introduction

This Cookie Policy explains how 3-Click Website Builder uses cookies and similar technologies to recognize you when you visit our website. It explains what these technologies are and why we use them, as well as your rights to control our use of them.

2. What are Cookies?

Cookies are small data files that are placed on your computer or mobile device when you visit a website. Cookies are widely used by website owners to make their websites work, or to work more efficiently, as well as to provide reporting information.

3. Types of Cookies We Use

3.1. Essential Cookies

These cookies are necessary for the website to function properly. They enable basic functions like page navigation and access to secure areas of the website.

3.2. Performance Cookies

These cookies help us understand how visitors interact with our website by collecting and reporting information anonymously.

3.3. Functionality Cookies

These cookies enable the website to provide enhanced functionality and personalization.

3.4. Targeting Cookies

These cookies are used to track visitors across websites to display relevant advertisements.

4. How We Use Cookies

We use cookies for the following purposes:

  • To remember your preferences and settings
  • To understand how you use our website
  • To improve our website's performance
  • To provide personalized content and advertisements
  • To analyze website traffic and usage patterns

5. Third-Party Cookies

We may use third-party services that use cookies, including:

  • Google Analytics for website analytics
  • Stripe for payment processing
  • Social media platforms for sharing features

6. Managing Cookies

You can control and/or delete cookies as you wish. You can delete all cookies that are already on your computer and you can set most browsers to prevent them from being placed.

To manage cookies, you can:

  • Adjust your browser settings
  • Use our cookie consent tool
  • Clear your browser cache

7. Cookie Duration

Cookies can remain on your computer or mobile device for different periods of time:

  • Session Cookies: These cookies exist only while your browser is open
  • Persistent Cookies: These cookies survive after your browser is closed
